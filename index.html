Interact with the square below to change the colors of the square!

Or type the first letter of the color you want the square to be!

Note: Available colors are Blue, Green, Red, Yellow & Orange.

Rules:

  • Green - Double Click Square
  • Blue - Enter Square
  • Yellow - Click Outside Square & Release Within Square
  • Red - Click Within Square & Release Outside of Square
  • Orange - Scroll Page
